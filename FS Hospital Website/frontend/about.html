About Mata Amritanandamayi and Amrita Hospitals

Mata Amritanandamayi Devi, lovingly known as Amma, is a world-renowned humanitarian and spiritual leader from India. Amma’s boundless compassion has inspired the creation of numerous charitable institutions, including the Amrita Hospitals network. Guided by Amma’s vision of selfless service, Amrita Hospitals are dedicated to providing advanced, affordable, and compassionate healthcare to all, regardless of background or means. The hospitals are recognized for their excellence in patient care, cutting-edge research, and commitment to serving society through a blend of modern medicine and holistic healing.

Amrita Hospitals Across India


Amrita Hospital, Kochi

One of Asia’s largest multi-speciality hospitals, renowned for advanced care and research.

Amrita Hospital, Faridabad

India’s largest private hospital, offering world-class healthcare and state-of-the-art facilities.

Amrita Hospital, Kalpetta

Serving the Wayanad region with quality healthcare and community outreach.

Amrita Hospital, Amritapuri

Providing compassionate care in Amma’s birthplace, with a focus on rural health.
