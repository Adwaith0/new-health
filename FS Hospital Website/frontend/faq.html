Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I book an appointment?


You can book an appointment by clicking the Book Appointment button on our homepage. After submitting your request, our team will reply and contact you soon to confirm your appointment.

2. What are your hospital visiting hours?


Our hospital is open from 8 AM to 8 PM, Monday to Saturday. Emergency services are available 24/7. For special visiting arrangements, please contact our helpdesk and we will get back to you as soon as possible.

3. Do you accept health insurance?


Yes, we accept major health insurance providers. Please check with our front desk for specific coverage details. If you have any queries, submit your details and our insurance team will reply and assist you soon.

4. Where can I find specialist doctors?


Visit our Meet Our Experts page to view specialist profiles and their availability. If you need help choosing a specialist, contact us and we will reply with recommendations.

5. How can I contact customer support?


You can reach us through our Contact Us page or call our helpline at +91 9876543210. We will reply and contact you soon to address your concerns.

6. I have a question not listed here. What should I do?


Please use our Contact Us form to submit your query. Our team will reply and get back to you as soon as possible.

Thank you for reaching out to Amrita Medicity. We value your queries and will reply and contact you soon!
